# Last World

## The Beginning

For thousands of years, humanity lived across countless worlds. Each world was sustained by ancient Relics forged from the primordial elements.

Then came **The Collapse**.

One by one, the worlds fell. Some were consumed by endless war, others by corruption, and others vanished without explanation.

The survivors fled.

Their final refuge became known as **Last World**—the only world believed to have survived.

The Relics that once connected the worlds were scattered across the land, becoming objects of legend. Kingdoms rose and fell in pursuit of them. Explorers searched forgotten ruins, mercenaries sold their blades to the highest bidder, pirates hunted treasure across dangerous seas, and powerful guilds sought to uncover their true power.

Some believe the Relics can restore what was lost.

Others seek them for absolute power.

And a few believe the greatest secret is not hidden within the Relics themselves... but in the truth behind **The Collapse**.

No one knows whether the other worlds were truly destroyed.

Only one certainty remains.

**This is the Last World.**
